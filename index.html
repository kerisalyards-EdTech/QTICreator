📚 How to import into Canvas ▼
  1. In Canvas, go to your course and click Settings in the left sidebar.
  2. Click "Import Course Content" at the top right.
  3. Under "Content Type", select "QTI .zip file".
  4. Click Choose File and select the downloaded .zip file.
  5. Click Import. Processing may take a few minutes.
  6. Once complete, your questions will appear in Quizzes → Question Banks.
  7. You can then add questions from the bank to any quiz.
